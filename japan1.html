New Doreen

"New Doreen" opened on 8th January,2001 and started as a Japanese restaurant. Later, it adds up more enjoyable cuisines and food lovers can enjoy Myanmar, Japanese, Thai, Chinese and European food now. Pregel ice-cream and freshly baked desserts are also offered. Moreover, Doreen’s breakfast menu is a great hit! Maximum (180) Customer.
